Rwanda is a small, landlocked country in East Africa known as the “Land of a Thousand Hills” because of its beautiful green landscapes. It is bordered by Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, and Democratic Republic of the Congo, with its capital city being Kigali. Rwanda is famous for its cleanliness, safety, and rapid development. It is also home to unique wildlife such as mountain gorillas found in Volcanoes National Park. The country has a rich culture, and its main languages include Kinyarwanda, English, and French
